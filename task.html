SACHIN TENDULKAR

1.Description :

       => Here is the overview of the great indian former cricketer who is also known as god cricket Sachin Tendulkar.

2.List Of Some Points About Sachin :

  1. He is the all-time highest run-scorer in both ODI and Test cricket with more than 18,000 runs and 15,000 runs, respectively.
  2. Tendulkar took up cricket at the age of eleven, made his Test match debut on 15 November 1989 against Pakistan in Karachi at the age of sixteen.
  3. Tendulkar played 664 international cricket matches in total, scoring 34,357 runs.
  4. He is the only player to score a century on debut in all three of his domestic first-class tournaments (the Ranji, Irani, and Duleep Trophies).

KAPIL DEV

1.Description :

       => Here is the overview of the great indian cricketer who has created a proud moment to india in 1984 Kapil dev.

2.List Of Some Points About Kapil Dev :

  1. Kapil Dev Ramlal Nikhanj born 6 January 1959 is an Indian former cricketer.
  2. Dev captained the Indian cricket team that won the 1983 Cricket World Cup,[4] and in the process became the first Indian captain to win the Cricket World Cup, and is still the youngest captain (at the age of 24) to win the World Cup for any team.
  3. On 11 March 2010, Dev was inducted into the ICC Cricket Hall of Fame.
  4. Kapil Dev made an impressive debut for Haryana in November 1975 against Punjab with a 6-wicket haul, restricting Punjab to just 63 runs and helping Haryana to victory.
